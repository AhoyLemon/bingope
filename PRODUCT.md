# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Five friends play a personal, one-day observation bingo game at the Minnesota State Fair on their phones. Their primary job is to quickly recognize a square, check its full meaning, and mark it while walking around the fairgrounds.

## Product Purpose

BINGOPE turns a shared day at the fair into a funny, low-friction game. Success is a card that remains legible in bright outdoor conditions, makes marking deliberate rather than accidental, and remembers each player's progress without accounts or a backend.

## Brand Personality

Specific, playful, sturdy. It should feel like a real fair-game artifact with a smart personal-project voice, not a generic bingo app or a noisy carnival poster.

## Anti-references

- Generic mobile bingo-app chrome: checkmarks, badges, progress dashboards, and stock game UI.
- Blank or overly minimal grids that hide the actual square copy.

## Design Principles

- Let the board do the work: all 25 prompts are present and readable at a glance.
- Marking must be visually decisive, physically evocative, and never obscure the underlying square.
- Make the ticket framing memorable, then keep surrounding chrome out of the way.
- Build for a phone being used outdoors, not a dashboard being admired at a desk.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA is the baseline. Squares are semantic buttons with visible focus, marking never relies only on color, the zoom view uses native dialog behavior, and reduced-motion preferences are respected.
