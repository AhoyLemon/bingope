## Idea log

Specific concepts that are parked or dead, so we don't re-pitch them. Banked ideas are in `_squares.ts`.

| Idea | Status | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| High-striker pity do-over ("swung so weakly the carnie let me try again") | Parked | Good bit, alt to the skill-game square. Outcome not fully in the player's control. |
| Princess Kay-specific sighting as a deliberate rare gem | Parked | Pinning to the single top princess vs any of ~12 is nearly unmarkable, but could be a chosen rare. |
| A second Sweet Martha's angle | Parked | Wanted "a couple" of Martha squares; only one banked. |
| Big Boar champion as an easy gimme | Parked | Chose the "find a non-champion hog" hunt instead; the champion is "hardly an action." |
| Plain Giant Slide ride | Parked | Superseded by the stone-faced version. Fallback only. |
| Turkey leg eaten "like a medieval king" | Rejected | Reads Renaissance Faire, not State Fair. |
| Generic "a crying adult" (no context) | Rejected | The guide's own unwinnable-fight / private-tragedy example. Reworked into "crying with an ice cream cone." |
| Carnie "I'd trust with my money" / "dripping in gold" | Rejected | Required interpreting an inner state. Became "wearing at least one piece of gold jewelry." |
| Baby in ear protection *at the Grandstand* | Rejected | Scuttled entirely (was also show-attendance dependent). |
| Sweet Martha's bucket "carried like a newborn" | Rejected | Arguable simile ("that's not like a newborn / yes it is"). |
| Cheese curd "one always falls to the ground" | Rejected | Advocates wasting food, and not actually true. Became the curd-sacrament do-it. |
